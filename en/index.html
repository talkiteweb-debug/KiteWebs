FAQ

The questions everyone asks on the first call

We don't like ambiguity. Honest answers below — and if your question isn't here, just call.

How long does it really take?
24 hours from the first call — guaranteed. Day 2 you see the first version, day 3 revisions, day 4 live with your domain. If we miss the deadline, you don't pay your first month of hosting.
Why only ₪990? Where's the catch?
No catch. We built our own tooling that speeds the process up — auto-generating copy from your Google details, designing with custom templates we developed. A traditional studio would put 4-6 weeks of person-hours on this. We do the same quality in less time, at a fair price.
Can I edit the site myself later?
Yes. We give you a simple panel where you can update photos, prices, hours, promotions — in 2 clicks. Bigger changes (like redesign) — send us a WhatsApp and we'll do it in a few hours.
I'm already on Facebook and Instagram — why do I need a website?
Customers searching for you on Google don't find your Facebook page — they find competitors. Google builds search results primarily on websites. A site from us connects to your Google profile and social pages automatically, so you show up everywhere people search.
What if I'm not happy?
30-day money-back guarantee, no questions asked. Full refund of the ₪990 plus first month. You're not locked into a yearly contract — leave any time, just say so, and we'll hand over the files.
Do you require upfront payment?
₪300 deposit to start (so we're aligned), the rest after the site is live and you've approved it 100%. The first call and the initial draft are free.
How do I know you're trustworthy? You're new.
Yes, we're a new company (2026) — that's why we fight for every customer. We have a 30-day guarantee, our offer is in writing, and you talk directly to me (Tal Hoch) on WhatsApp at every stage. No support queues, no "the manager isn't here."
What if I already have a domain or an old site?
Great — we migrate your domain at no extra cost. If you have an old site that doesn't work well, we take your existing content and build something that works, from scratch. All included in the ₪990.
