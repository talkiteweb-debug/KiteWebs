Accessibility Statement

Last updated: May 27, 2026

Our commitment to accessibility

KiteWeb believes the internet should be accessible to everyone — regardless of physical, sensory, or cognitive ability. This site was designed and built to comply with Israeli accessibility law, including the Equal Rights for People with Disabilities Regulations (Service Accessibility Adjustments), 2013 and Israeli Standard 5568 at Level AA (equivalent to WCAG 2.0 AA).

Accessibility options on this site

An accessibility menu is available on every page (the icon at the bottom-right), offering:

  • Increase and decrease text size
  • High-contrast mode
  • Link highlighting
  • Reset to defaults

Your settings are saved automatically and will persist across visits.

Other accessibility features

  • Full keyboard navigation — every interactive element is reachable with Tab
  • Clear visual focus indicator on every link and button
  • "Skip to main content" link appears when navigating by keyboard
  • ARIA labels on all controls and action buttons
  • Alt text on meaningful images
  • Color contrast meeting AA requirements (4.5:1 for normal text, 3:1 for large text)
  • Screen reader compatibility (VoiceOver, NVDA, JAWS)
  • Full RTL support for Hebrew content
  • Clean, readable typography with no disruptive animations

Known limitations

We continuously work to improve accessibility. If you find content that is not properly accessible, please report it to our accessibility coordinator and we'll address it promptly.
