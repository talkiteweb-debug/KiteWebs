Frequently asked

Questions we hear on every first call.

If yours isn't here, just call — we're happy to answer anything before you commit to working with us.

How long does it take to build a website?
Most websites go live within 7-14 days. Single-page landing sites can be live in 3-5 days. Complex multi-page sites with custom features take 2-4 weeks. You get a clear timeline on the first call — no guessing.
How much does a website cost?
Depends on scope. Single-page landing sites start at ₪990 / about $280. Multi-page business sites run ₪2,500-5,000. Complex platforms quoted per project. Every quote is fixed-price — no surprise invoices later.
What kind of automations can you build?
Lead follow-ups (auto-reply within seconds), online booking, email sequences, CRM connections, form-to-database flows, WhatsApp bots, SMS reminders, internal team workflows, reporting dashboards. If a human is doing the same task repeatedly — we can probably automate it.
Do you run ongoing marketing campaigns or just setup?
Both. We can build the landing page and hand it off, or run the entire campaign — paid ads, A/B tests, email sequences, conversion tracking, weekly reporting. Most clients start with setup and add ongoing management once they see results.
Do you work with businesses outside Israel?
Yes. We currently work with clients in Israel, the US, and Europe. We support both English and Hebrew — and we're happy to work across time zones. Most communication happens over WhatsApp, email, and scheduled calls.
What if I'm not happy with the work?
We work in checkpoints — you approve each milestone before we move to the next. If we miss the target at any checkpoint, we redo it at no charge. Most clients are happy with v1; if not, we iterate until they are.
What's included in monthly support?
Hosting, security updates, SSL renewal, small content edits (we'll update text or photos for you), basic SEO monitoring, and direct support over WhatsApp and email. Anything more substantial — new pages, redesigns, new automations — is quoted separately.
How do you measure marketing results?
We wire up Google Analytics, conversion tracking, ad pixels, and our own dashboards from day one. You get a weekly or monthly report showing traffic, conversion rate, cost per lead, and ROI. No vanity metrics — only the numbers that tie to revenue.
